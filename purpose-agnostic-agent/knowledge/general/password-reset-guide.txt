Password Reset Guide

How to Reset Your Password

Method 1: Using the "Forgot Password" Link
1. Go to the login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Click "Send Reset Link"
5. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
6. Click the reset link in the email (valid for 24 hours)
7. Enter your new password (must be at least 8 characters)
8. Confirm your new password
9. Click "Reset Password"
10. You'll be redirected to the login page

Method 2: Through Account Settings (If Logged In)
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Account Settings" or "Profile"
3. Click on "Security" or "Password" tab
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter your new password
6. Confirm your new password
7. Click "Save Changes"

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot be the same as your last 3 passwords
- Cannot contain your username or email

Troubleshooting:

Problem: Didn't receive reset email
Solutions:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Wait 5-10 minutes (emails may be delayed)
- Try requesting another reset link
- Contact support if still not received

Problem: Reset link expired
Solution:
- Request a new reset link (links expire after 24 hours)

Problem: New password not accepted
Solution:
- Ensure password meets all requirements
- Avoid common passwords
- Try a different password

Security Tips:
1. Use a unique password for each account
2. Use a password manager
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
4. Never share your password
5. Change passwords regularly (every 3-6 months)
6. Don't use personal information in passwords
7. Log out from shared devices

Password Reset Guide

To reset your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Click the link in the email
6. Enter your new password (must be at least 8 characters)
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click "Reset Password"

Your password has now been reset and you can log in with your new credentials.

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
